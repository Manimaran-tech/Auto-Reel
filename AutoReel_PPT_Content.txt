SLIDE 2 — OUR SOLUTION
================================================================================

Title:
    Introducing AutoReel AI

One-Liner:
    "We built an AI agent that turns any standard e-commerce URL into a 
     fully edited, voice-acted, ready-to-post video ad in under 30 seconds."

What It Actually Does:
    1. Scrapes the web for product images, pricing, and features.
    2. Writes a highly-converting viral script tailored to the product.
    3. Generates human-like voiceover audio.
    4. Automatically edits the video (adds motion, audio sync, and pop-up subtitles).
    5. Pushes the final video directly to Instagram Drafts.

The "Anti-Shadowban" Innovation:
    Most competing tools use official APIs to automatically post videos. 
    Instagram's algorithm severely punishes (shadowbans) API-posted content.
    
    Our Solution: AutoReel uses UI Automation (OpenClaw) to mimic human 
    clicks and place the video safely into "Drafts". The human simply clicks
    "Post". Organic reach stays at 100%.

SLIDE 4 — THE ENGINEERING WOW FACTOR
================================================================================

Title:
    Building a Rendering Engine from Scratch

Why this is technically impressive:
    We did not just plug a URL into an expensive third-party video API like 
    HeyGen or Runway. We built the video compositor entirely in code.

    • Algorithmic Editing: The Python backend mathematically calculates 
      audio duration, scales the images, and applies Ken Burns Zoom effects
      so static pictures look like cinematic video.
    • Subtitle Synchronization: The hardest part of video AI. We sync words
      dynamically so they pop on screen exactly as the voiceover speaks them,
      mimicking the high-retention "TikTok/Reels" style.

    Everything renders locally. It requires zero subscription fees, making
    it infinitely scalable for a startup.
